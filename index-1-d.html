What is Magazine and newspaper Desigening?

There is not much difference between postcards and flyers from a print perspective. Typically, postcards are printed on both sides, in contrast to flyers and are different sizes. However, we can make (almost) anything, so write/call or stop by one of our stores and we'll find the perfect solution to cater to your needs.

  • Magazine

    • The traditional sizes for postcards are A5, A6 and A7 but we can also print postcards in other sizes if desired. We can of course print on both sides to the edge in black and white or colour. The surface can also be treated with matt lamination, partial lacquer print etc. and you can select amoung an array of paper thicknesses.

  • Newspaper

    • A flyer is typically used as a flyer, invitation, coupon, ticket, menus etc. Flyers are available with printing on one or both sides and may be in black and white or colour.
    Flyers are effective when you want to get your message out to many people.
